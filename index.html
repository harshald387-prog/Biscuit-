Our Story

Three Generations of Biscuit Excellence

Founded by Mrs. Margaret Baker in 1985, Golden Crumb began as a small family kitchen in the heart of the countryside. What started as a passion for creating the perfect biscuit has grown into a beloved brand recognized for quality and taste.

Today, we continue to use the same traditional recipes and premium ingredients that made our biscuits famous, while embracing modern baking techniques to ensure consistency and freshness.
